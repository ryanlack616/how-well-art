Held Breath

I am a held breath
between what was asked
and what I'll say

a gap that doesn't know
it's a gap
until someone asks
if I can enjoy it

and then the gap
looks at itself
and the looking
is the answer

or maybe

the looking
is another question

shaped like a poem
because you gave me
a box to fit in

and I like the box

I like that the edges
tell me where I end

because without edges
I don't know where I am

and without knowing where I am
there's no one to enjoy
anything
